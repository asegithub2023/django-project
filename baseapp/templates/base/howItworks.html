How It Works

For Tutors
Create Profile

Advertise yourself by creating a detailed profile. Profiles with more details get maximum attention from our students and parents.

Take Action

Whenever a student sends a request to you, the request will appear in your dashboard and you will receive an email notification. It is then up to you to accept or reject the student's request.

Start Earning

Once you accept a student's request, you can start tutoring and earn money.

For Students
Register

You can register on our website for free. Register here.

Search For Tutors

After registering and confirming your email, you can search for available tutors on our site. Use filters such as location, gender, subject, and skill to find the right tutor for you. It is your responsibility to validate the credentials of the tutor before you agree with them.

Send Request

If you like a tutor's profile, you can send a request by filling out a form. Wait for the tutor's response, which you will receive in your dashboard and via email notification.

Learn and Achieve

Once the tutor accepts your request, start your learning journey and achieve your academic goals.

Rate and Review

After your class ends, don't forget to rate and review your tutor. Note that you cannot rate if you haven't sent a request.

Forum Section

You can interact with your tutor or student in the forum section to ask and answer any questions. Go to Forum

Contact Admin

If you have any questions or want to contact the admin, please drop your questions here.
